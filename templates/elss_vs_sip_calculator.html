Equity Linked Savings Scheme (ELSS) is a type of mutual fund that invests primarily in equity and equity-related instruments. It offers tax benefits under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act with a 3-year lock-in period.

ELSS investments are eligible for tax deduction up to â‚¹1.5 lakh per year under Section 80C. This calculator shows the additional benefit you get from tax savings compared to regular SIP investments.

ðŸ”’ Lock-in Period

ELSS funds have a mandatory 3-year lock-in period, which is the shortest among all tax-saving investments under Section 80C. Regular SIP investments have no lock-in period.

Both ELSS and regular equity SIPs carry similar market risks as they invest in equity. ELSS funds may have slightly higher returns due to their focused investment approach.
